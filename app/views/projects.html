hashlib++

I started hashlib++ in 2007 as a simple and very easy to use wrapper library to create cryptographic checksums in C++. Over the years alot of projects made use of hashlib++, where Bohemia Interactive's ARMA II is probably the most popular. hashlib++ is written in plain C++ and should work with every compiler and platform. The library is released under the BSD-license and therefore free software.

http://hashlib2plus.sourceforge.net
